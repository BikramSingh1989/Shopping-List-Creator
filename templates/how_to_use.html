How to Use the Inventory App

  1. Register or log in to your account.
  2. Add items with quantity, category, par level, and optional expiration date.
  3. Items below par level will appear in your Shopping List automatically.
  4. Edit or delete individual items with ✏️ and 🗑️ or clear all with 🧹.
  5. Toggle between light, dark, or sepia mode using the 🌗 button.
  6. Use on mobile or desktop — the layout is fully responsive and accessible.

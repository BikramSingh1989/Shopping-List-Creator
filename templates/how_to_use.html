How to Use the Inventory App

  1. Register or log in to your account.
  2. Add items with quantity, par level, and optional expiration date.
  3. Items below par level will show in your Shopping List automatically.
  4. Delete individual items with 🗑️ or clear the full inventory with 🧹.
  5. Toggle between light/dark mode using the 🌗 button.
  6. Use on mobile or desktop — the layout is fully responsive.
